We're happy to announce that the Public Library of Science has launched the first  PLoS: Currents section, PLoS: Currents Disasters on a self-hosted WordPress site using the Annotum platform.

PLoS took the Annotum Base theme and extended both the look and feel and the functionality - it's a shining example of what you can do for your own open-access journal.

Congratulations to PLoS and we're looking forward to more sections launching soon.  You can visit the new section at http://currents.plos.org/disasters.
