Welcome to the new Annotum site!  Feel free to explore the information here, and don't forget to follow us on Twitter (@annotum) or subscribe to site updates using the button on the right.  I'll soon be deprecating the old info.annotum.org for anyone who was using it (yes, both of you).

We're just starting development this week, working through the intricacies of workflow and permissions.  The workflow model is fairly simple, with just a few actions (and the resulting state in parentheses)

  1. Create  (state: draft)
  2. Submit (state: in review)
  3. Accept/revise/reject (state: accepted | draft | rejected)
  4. Publish
