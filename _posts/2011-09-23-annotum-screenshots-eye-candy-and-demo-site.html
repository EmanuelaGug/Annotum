Eye Candy - Detailed Walkthrough

As part of our preparation for JATS-CON (see below) we have created a detailed walkthrough of Annotum's features.  You'll find the walkthrough right here, or click "Screenshots" in the right navigation menu.

Updated Demo Site

The Annotum team is pleased to announce the launch of the Annotum Demonstration site at http://demo.annotum.org.  You can visit the demo site to see how an Annotum-based journal may look.  We don't yet allow test accounts on the demo site but those will come with the beta launch (real soon now).

JATS-CON

We're also preparing for our upcoming presentation at JATS-Con, the Journal Article Tag Suite conference hosted by the National Library of Medicine (NLM).  We'll be showing a brief demo of the latest Annotum code.
