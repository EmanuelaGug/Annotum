This Tuesday marks the three-month anniversary of the launch of Annotum, and to date close to 40,000 people have downloaded the theme via WordPress.org. That's on top of more than 3,000 sites running the Annotum Theme on WordPress.com.

To celebrate the anniversary, and in preparation of the release of Annotum v1.02 (with significant bug fixes and even some nice new features), we'd love to hear from anyone and everyone using Annotum, either for a test or for an actual journal. Please contact us or simply post a comment to this post with a link to your site.

Once we have a few examples, we'll create a showcase of Annotum-based journals for you to peruse.

And if you just can't wait for the official release of v1.02, feel free to grab it from GitHub - look for the latest "tag" to download.

As always, if you need any help setting up your Annotum site, or want to inquire about customizing Annotum, feel free to let us know.
