Beta!

We're pleased to announce the release of Beta 1 of Annotum. This release offers a significant increase in functionality over our alpha release from some months ago, and apart from a few known issues, it is essentially feature complete.

So, check out the release notes, download the theme files, or try out the stand-alone installers for Windows and Mac OS X. Feel free to post any questions in the comments below, or let us know if you find any bugs or other issues.
