Dear Sir,

Website: www.myopensourcestore.com is a platform started with the following objectives:

• Creating awareness of the availability of good quality open source software.
• Ensure Consultation, Installation Support and configuration
• Provide a minimum quality guarantee for approved products
We intend to tie up with core team of each software to provide the core support services. The initial first level support may be provide by the local team or the core team itself. The final objective is to popularize the usage of open source software by ensuring quality software and support at competitive rates.

We have identified your organization as one of the companies’ active in the open source area. We would like to include “__________” into our website and provide a link to your download area. We would also offer your services like installation, configuration etc. on the web-site provided by a support organization, which could be yours. An agent’s commission for the same would be retained by www.myopensourcestore.com and rest passed on to you, if you are providing the service and support.

The details of the web-site will be seen at by www.myopensourcestore.com. In short:

 We would get a link to your download area
 You could promote your products/services on the web-site

We also intend to use the funds generated from the web-site for common causes to promote opensource activities. Please give us a feedback on the initial interest in participating in a movement to popularize opensource initiatives, so that we could proceed with further formalities.
