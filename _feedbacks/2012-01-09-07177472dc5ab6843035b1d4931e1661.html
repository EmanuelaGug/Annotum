Dear Sirs

Subject: Would you be able to kindly help us?

We’re looking for a highly experienced web development company with design and extensive back-end coding skills onboard that would be willing to support the development of our platform, free of charge.

Not much then!

Champions Club Community is an organisation dedicated to helping all people recognise and fulfil their potential, irrespective or where or how they started out in
life – being the difference

It’s an amazing story – over 107 people so far have come together as volunteers, to give of their skills and talents to help others. No one is being paid directly to do this, but there is a share scheme in place to reward people’s contribution. You can read more about us at;

http://championsclubcommunity.com/my-community/files/2011/12/People-Proof-11.pdf
We’re building a global life improvement / personal development platform for the benefit of others, framed around Abraham Maslow’s Hierarchy of needs (from the basic requirements of food and shelter through to self-actualisation).

A key part of this is what we are calling “The Global Noticeboard”, whereby those able to give, (e.g. food, clothes, money, computers, books, skills, coaching, mentoring etc), are matched with those in need, direct. 100% of everything we do goes to helping others. There are no administrative deductions. It’s a new model for charitable giving.

We need help and resources to continue on this amazing journey which is the reason we are approaching you. By joining with us we believe you can have a large beneficial effect on what we are doing. Where we think this could be a good fit with you, is perhaps under Sustainability (People, Planet, Profit) and also Corporate Social Responsibility.

How can you help? Well first and foremost we would simply like to come and chat and share with you what we are doing in greater detail. We think it will be an interesting meeting!

Other key partners we are currently in discussion with for support in other ways include; Barclays, Northern Trust, Virgin Media, Reading Borough Council, Reigate and Banstead Borough Council, First Capital Connect, Eversheds Solicitors and Oracle.
Many Organisations’ employees are already contributing in various ways and they are themselves now “Being the Difference”.

Being the difference in the lives of others is really important for sure, but what people find when they do this is that they also become the difference in their own lives too. By giving and making a contribution in this way they end up having a richer, more meaningful life themselves.

Imagine a world where we each give something back without any thought or regard to our own personal gain or profit. This is exactly what we are building. A global “giving” platform, where those in need are able to connect directly with those who are able to give something, however great or small this might be.

Many thousands of people giving small amounts of course adds up to being a massive difference in the lives of others.

In building Champions Club Community we have been guided by the Margaret Mead statement; “Never doubt that a small group of thoughtful and committed people can change the world, indeed it’s the only thing that ever does”.

Our request is simply that you kindly agree to meet with us to explore whether you might be able to play a role in helping us build the Global Noticeboard and our extended Community.

Thank you. We look forward to hearing back from you.

Best Wishes,

Guy Insull
