Greetings from Argentina. I am a knol author and work with the migration to your site.
I am journalist an writer in my country and I write about several items.
My works are divided into several blogs Annotum by Topic.
I have great hope in this project.
I make two suggestions.
Articles that meet your requirements of quality in Annotum can have no limit in space and images. Many scientists and teachers do not have a credit card to pay for an expansion of space.
To facilitate, as Knol, a community of authors, highlighting the best works and most popular works by language and/or region.. Each author is recognized as such by all of his works that can be in several blogs in Annotum.
I wish the best for those involved in this important project.
Thank you for this communication. Authors want to speak; their voice is important and we, the Knol authors, had not anwsers in Knol a lot of time. Other authors can tell if this is true.

Alberto Auné
