Hello

I have just discovered annotum. I am a professor who recently joined a Thai university and I already run an academic journal. I have just downloaded the latest version of the annotum theme and installed it on WP3.1.3.

I understand that the system is not fully developed yet but there are some things which do not seem to be operational and I wonder if I am doing something wrong or if it simply is not ready yet.

Here are three things I am concerned about:

(a) It is not clear how one can upload and format articles other than in fckeditor.

(b) While workflow is turned on, I cannot see the workflow sequencing for articles submitted for publication.

(c) The PDF/XML/email etc... functions do not seem to work at this stage

(d) It is not clear how/where information re address of contributors etc is held and displayed (in the usual academic format at the top of articles).

I am wondering about these partly because I am not familiar with wordpress and, as I said, wondering what I am doing wrong.

I really like what I have seen so far and look forward to monitoring and eventually using Annotum.

best wishes
Andrew Lian
