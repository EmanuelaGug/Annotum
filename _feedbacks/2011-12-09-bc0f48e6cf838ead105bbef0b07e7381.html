As part of the on-going investigation into the Annotum theme over on Worpress I am advised to let you know of any more problems I encounter with using the theme.

I have noticed that titles within the body of the article render differently. On some articles the titles are just bold plain text, but on some articles the titles are italic bold and they have a neat underline towards the finish. I thought that this issue might be because I have copied and pasted the title from Microsoft Word, but I copied the italic title including the title box over to an article with only bold plain-text titles within it, and it was rendered as bold plain text.

Thanks.
