Dear Annotum-Team

your software Annotum was added into the CARPET catalogue.

http://www.carpet-project.net/en/catalogue/detail-view/carpet/annotum/

If you don't agree with us publishing this information or the information provided
are incomplete or wrong, please let us know.

What is CARPET?
With the free-to-use infrastructure of CARPET you will improve the visibility of your
developments and promote their sustainable subsequent use. Existing e-publishing
technologies are categorically collected in the CARPET catalogue and presented
in a central location to a professional audience.

We are currently working on possibilities for developers to manage their content and
projects by themselves: CARPET is intended as an infrastructure tool to connect
developers with their users, merging modern technology like blogs, forums and
wikis into one interface.

CARPET is established as a durable center of competence for technical solutions
of electronic publishing and promotes the sustainable use of existing
projects by avoiding duplicate developments. CARPET is a noncommercial
cooperation between the Humboldt University zu Berlin, the Göttingen State and
University Library and the Max Planck Digital Library, funded by the German
Research Foundation (DFG).

We would like to welcome you and your community on www.carpet-project.net.
