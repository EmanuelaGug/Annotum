The details of why not all combinations of browsers/operating-systems work with Annotum don't matter, since I've aready discussed the issue with WordPress, and they have no plans to support old stuff, even when it has been proved to be more secure on the Internet than newer stuff.

The thing that doesn't work is the ability to edit the "Body" text of an Annotum Article, which wants to invoke some non-existing fancy browser or OS feature. However, editing the Body text of an Annotum Post works fine.

Well, since WordPress already has the tools that can convert Knols into Annotum Articles; it should be simple to click a button on an Annotum Post Body, and have WordPress convert it into an Annotum Article Body. Then the browser/operating-system (which was fine for creating Knols in the first place, remember!) doesn't matter.

Thank you!
