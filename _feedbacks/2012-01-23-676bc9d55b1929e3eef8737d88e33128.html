I'm very interested in the idea of Annotum. There are a few things that currently seem to be missing: the ability to upload a custom PDF associated with an article; the ability to associate articles with certain issues; and the ability to send an article to someone for a blind review.

To explain this, our standard workflow is to have someone email us an article, usually in Microsoft Word format; have the article reviewed over a number of months, usually by ancient professors who do not want another account; and then have it typeset once it has been edited. We're really interested in the idea of having a better electronic version of our publication (and even making this the primary format), but we do need some way to be able to have people review an article without knowing the author's name. The ideal thing, I think, would be to have a function that would allow an anonymous PDF to be generated during the review process that could then be sent to the reviewer.

Thanks for your work on this!

Andrew Dunning
University of Toronto
