Dear Sir/Madam

My name is Thomas King, the research assistant for the Scholarly Communication in Africa Programme (SCAP). We are extremely interested in Annotum, and would love to begin talking with the administrators and developers about the scope of this new platform for scholarly communication and publishing.

SCAP is an IDRC-funded programme exploring the realm of scholarly communications in the sub-Saharan African context, and part of the ambit of the programme is to explore efficient web-based platforms for Open Access Research. We understand that while many scholars internationally support Open Access as a theoretical model, wide-scale uptake of Open Access will only occur when the tools used to promote open research are net time savers for researchers, and must thus be user- and consumer-friendly, efficient and targeted to as wide a scholarly audience as possible.

We would love to start talking to you about the possibility of using Annotum in the sub-Saharan publishing arena, which has little global representation despite the quantity and quality of original research being produced.

We look forward to hearing from you soon, and would like to express our appreciation that your project exists and is addressing such an urgent scholarly need.

Yours sincerely
- Thomas King
